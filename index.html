SYNC TIME ~30–80s
PROTOCOL MSP / USB
LAPTOP NOT REQUIRED
RANGE WI-FI HOTSPOT

// PROCEDURE

01

Flash full.

FC stops logging mid-session. Land your quad.

02

Plug in.

Connect FC to the Pi Zero W via OTG cable (solid LED = ready). Sync starts automatically.

03

LED: done.

~30–80 seconds. Verified. Flash erased. Unplug the FC.

04

Fly again.

Repeat all session. Logs timestamped on the Pi — ready at home.

BEFORE

Laptop.
Or dongle.

  • FC flash full — session interrupted
  • Find a laptop, open Configurator, cable up
  • — OR — buy an expensive USB dongle
  • Either way: stop flying, leave the field

NOW

Pi Zero W.
In your bag.

  • FC flash full — land for 30 seconds
  • Plug into the Pi, wait for the LED
  • Fly again — repeat as many times as needed
  • Logs timestamped and ready at home
